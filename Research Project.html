Social Networking

Summary

Social Networking is a Internet-based media and a Virtual Community. Social networking is used as a tool for businesses and for social reasons. They use there social media presence to increase conversion rates a.k.a. Sales. Others use social networking in order to connect with friends and family to see what they are up to. There are many disadvantages but quite a bit of advantages as well. Some disadvantages could be, someone could hack into your account and steal your information. Some advantages could be increasing sales if you are a business owner.
